What Kind of Chain Do We Sell?
We sell pretty much any kind of rigging chain you could want. One thing our customers love most is that we sell much of our chain by the foot, while offering deeper discounts when buying full drums. This includes Domestic Grade 100 Lifting Chain, in all the popular sizes. But if you want the strongest lifting chain available, then be sure to check out our Pewag Blue Grade 120 Lifting Chain. We also offer Grade 70 Tie Down Chain in both NACM Standard Link and ASTM Short Link, as well as lower grades of chain in various finishes.
